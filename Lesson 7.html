The -as ending of verbs - the present tense - negative sentences

ne - not

Ŝi amas la knabon.

She loves the boy.
She is loving the boy.
She does love the boy.

Ŝi ne amas la knabon.

She is not loving the boy.
She does not love the boy.

ne goes just before the verb.

The -ul- suffix

-ul- - individual, person

juna - young
junulo - a youth

bona - good
bonulo - a good person

rapida - fast
rapidulo - a speedster

The -ist- suffix

-ist- - professional, or someone who habitually does

dento - tooth
dentisto - dentist

programi - to program
