The -os ending of verbs - the future tense - negative sentences

Ŝi amos la knabon.

She will love the boy.

Ŝi ne amos la knabon.

She will not loving the boy.

ne goes just before the verb, just as for present and past tenses.

ri

ri - he/she or him/her    (gender-neutral third person singular)
ria - his/her

ri is not officially recognized, but it has become rapidly more popular since 2010.

  • when you don't know the gender
  • when you refer to a non-binary gender person
