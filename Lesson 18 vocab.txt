# correlatives

kiel - in what way, how
tiel - in that way, so
ĉi tiel - in this way
iel - in some way, somehow
ĉiel - in every way
neniel - in no way

ajn - -ever (with ki- words), any (with i- words)


# adverbs

malpli - less (used with ol)
malplej - least (used with el)


# pronouns

ri - he/she (subject) /  him/her (object of preposition)    (gender-neutral third person singular)
rin - him/her (object of verb)


# possessive adjectives

ria - his/her
