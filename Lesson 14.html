The -is ending of verbs - the past tense - yes or no questions

compare Lesson 8, present tense with ĉu

Ŝi amis la knabon.

She loved the boy.
She was loving the boy.
She did love the boy.

Ĉu ŝi amis la knabon?

Was she loving the boy?
Did she love the boy?

ĉu goes at the start of the sentence.
