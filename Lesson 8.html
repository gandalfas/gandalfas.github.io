jes and ne

jes - yes
ne - no

ĉu - yes or no questions

Ŝi amas la knabon.

She loves the boy.
She is loving the boy.
She does love the boy.

Ĉu ŝi amas la knabon?

Is she loving the boy?
Does she love the boy?

ĉu goes at the start of the sentence.

When we turn a statement into a question...
In English, we move does or is to the beginning of the sentence.
In Esperanto, no words move.
