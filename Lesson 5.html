The sound of e in Esperanto

it is not actually the sound of e in get   IPA:    /ɛ/   [epsilon]
it is higher, as in French été                  IPA:    /e/
it is not the sound of a in play               IPA:    /eɪ/  [epsilon cap-I]

however 'e' in get will be fine.
but as we have seen, bone sounds better as /e/  :)

The -et- suffix

-et- - much smaller - not the same as malgranda

domo - house
dometo - cottage

varma - hot
varmeta - lukewarm

dormi - to sleep
dormeti - to doze

ridi - to laugh
rideti - to smile
