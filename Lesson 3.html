kio and tio (correlatives)

kio - what (thing)
tio - that (thing)

The -ar- suffix

-ar- - collection, group

arbo - tree
arbaro - forest

libro - book
libraro - book collection

homo - person
homaro - humanity

hundo - dog
hundaro - pack of dogs

birdo - bird
birdaro - flock of birds

bovo - cow
bovaro - herd of cows
