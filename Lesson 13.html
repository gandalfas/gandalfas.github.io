The -is ending of verbs - the past tense - negative sentences

compare Lesson 7, present tense with ne

Ŝi amis la knabon.

She loved the boy.
She was loving the boy.
She did love the boy.

Ŝi ne amis la knabon.

She was not loving the boy.
She did not love the boy.

ne goes just before the verb, just as for -as the present tense.

Practice - 3 English meanings - 2 English meanings
