demandi vs peti

  • demandi - to ask (a question)
  • peti - to petition, to request

Dummy Subjects

For some verbs, there is no actor - a person or animal does not 'rain'.  Possibly the clouds rain?

In English, we require a subject for every verb.  So for the above, we use a dummy subject, it.
In Esperanto, there is no subject.

Pluvas. - It is raining.  It rains.
Pluvis hieraŭ. - It rained yesterday.

Neĝas. - It is snowing.
Neĝos morgaŭ. - It will snow tomorrow.

Here is another case:  there.

There are people who say...
Estas homoj kiu diras...

Once there was a princess...
Iam estis princino...                  -- iam - at some time
