# adverbs

hodiaŭ - today
hieraŭ - yesterday
morgaŭ - tomorrow
nun - now
nuna - present, current ("nowish")
apenaŭ - barely
tre - very
jam - already
supre - above, up
malsupre - below, down
baldaŭ - soon
eĉ - even
ja - indeed
bele - beautifully
feliĉe - happily
forte - strongly
nove - newly
kontente - contentedly
libere - freely
bone - well - as in well done.  I fare well.  (you would think it was "good/ly", but no - 'well' is the adverb form of 'good' in English!)

# correlatives

kie - where (at which place)
tie - there (at that place)
