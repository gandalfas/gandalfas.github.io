# phrases

Dankon! - Thank you!
Nedankinde! - You're welcome!  (not worthy of thanks)


# prepositions

sur - on
al - to
de - from, since, by, of (possession)


# verbs

havi - to have
ami - to love
fari - to do, to make
voli - to want
kisi - to kiss
kuiri - to cook
manĝi - to eat
diri - to say, to tell
vidi - to see


# correlatives

kiu /noun/ - which /noun/, which /noun/ specifically
tiu /noun/ - that /noun/, that /noun/ specifically

kiu (with no noun) - which one, which one specifically (or who, if you know it's a person)
tiu (with no noun) - that one, that one specifically
