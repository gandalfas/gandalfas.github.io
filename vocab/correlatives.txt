kio - what (thing), in a general way
tio - that (thing), in a general way
ĉi tio - this (thing), in a general way
io - something
ĉio - everything
nenio - nothing

kiu /noun/ - which /noun/, which /noun/ specifically
tiu /noun/ - that /noun/, that /noun/ specifically
ĉi tiu /noun/ - this /noun/, this /noun/ specifically
iu /noun/ - some /noun/
ĉiu /noun/ - every /noun/
neniu /noun/ - no /noun/

kiu (with no noun) - which one, which one specifically (or who, if you know it's a person)
tiu (with no noun) - that one, that one specifically
ĉi tiu (with no noun) - this one, this one specifically
iu (with no noun) - someone
ĉiu (with no noun) - everyone
neniu (with no noun) - no one

kie - where (which place)
tie - there (that place)
ĉi tie - here (this place)
ie - somewhere
ĉie - everywhere
nenie - nowhere
