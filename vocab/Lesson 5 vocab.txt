pri - about
por - for
sub - under, below
dum - during (while, as a conjunction)
kun - with
sen - without
post - after, behind    [post meridiem "PM"]
antaŭ - before   [ante meridiem "AM"]
super - over, above
apud - beside
tra - through
ĉirkaŭ - around
el - out of
ekster - outside
je - on (date), at (time), -or- a non-specific preposition
per - by (by means of)
