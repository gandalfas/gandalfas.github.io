# prepositions

sur - on
ad+ - to ~ al+
de - from (not: since, by, of/possession)


# verbs

havas - have/has
amas - loves
facas+ - does or makes
volas - wants
kisas - kisses
koquas+ - cooks ~ kuiras+
manjas - eats
dicas+ - says, tells ~ diras+
vidas - sees


# correlatives

qua+ /noun/ - which /noun/, which /noun/ specifically ~ kiu+ 
ta+  noun/ - that /noun/, that /noun/ specifically ~ tiu+

qua+ (with no noun) - who, which one (= person) ~ kiu+
ta+ (with no noun) - that one (= person) ~ tiu+
