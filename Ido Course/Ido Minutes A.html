Ido Minute 7

move from male-centered to gender-neutral

domo
domego                                   mansion is a type of house (a supergrand house)
dometo                                    cottage is a type of house

patro - patrino                         is a mother a type of father?  no.
patro - matro

viro - virino                            is a woman is a type of man?  no.
viro - muliero

gefrato - frato - fratino            is a sister a type of brother?   a female brother?   no.
frato  - fratulo - fratino        frato - sibling            fratulo - brother            fratino - sister

genevo - nevo - nevino           is a niece is a type of nephew?  no.
nevo  - nevulo - nevino         nevo - nephew-or-niece            nevulo - nephew            nevino - niece
